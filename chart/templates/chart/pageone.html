    HOW IT WORKS?

    Do you have a diary?

    Well if you have one you should write on it too and if you don't have any then it's great. The first step is to note down your tasks that you're going to do for each day. Make sure to write them in a bit of a concise manner.

    Read the first step first.

    Now you should try to perform really well and complete these tasks. Once you complete then now you have given a score to your performance based on how happy and satisfied you feel(You can set your own parameters).

    On scale of 1 to 10, the importance of this step is 11.

    The purpose of all this is to help you analyze how you perform in day to day tasks and also what things or habits work for you.

    Time to popup some smile ;)

    Now you have to enter which day is it? e.g Day 1. Here we are calling the performance score as Happiness score (Enter a value between 1 to 10). And just briefly describe your tasks like dancing, swimming. Once you add data, click on the results to see the graph between Day(x-axis) and Your Happiness Score (y-axis). Through this graph you can track your performance and if you want to analyze a particular day(e.g day on which the graph is at maximum) you have to scroll down below and see the data in the table below.
